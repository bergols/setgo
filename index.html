What is SelfGo?

SelfGo is a self-service kiosk designed to improve customer experiences. Whether you run a small business, a local market stand, or a café, SelfGo helps customers order, pay, and go — all on their own, with speed and convenience.

Key Features

  • Touchscreen interface for easy navigation
  • Voice command support for accessibility
  • Multi-language options
  • Customizable for any type of business
  • Integration with QR code and digital payments

Looking Ahead

  • Facial recognition for personalized service
  • AI assistant for interactive support
  • Solar-powered version for outdoor use
  • Real-time customer feedback and analytics

Real-World Inspiration

Major brands like McDonald’s and Walmart use kiosk tech to improve service. Studies show that 67% of customers prefer self-service over waiting for a human assistant.
